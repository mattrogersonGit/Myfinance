Tips & tricks
📥
Importing transactions
Export a CSV from your bank's internet banking and import it here. Duplicates are detected automatically.
🏷️
Categorising
Tap the category pill on any transaction to change it. A rule is automatically saved so future transactions from the same place are categorised for you.
📊
Setting budgets
In Categories & Budgets, tap a category's emoji icon to see your average monthly spend over 3, 6 and 12 months. Hit Use to set it as your budget.
📅
Browsing past months
Use the ‹ › arrows at the top of Overview and Transactions to navigate between months.
✏️
Editing transactions
Tap a transaction description to rename it. This also lets you clean up messy bank reference text.
📋
Auto-rules
Rules automatically categorise transactions matching a keyword. You can view, edit or delete them under Auto-categorisation rules in Settings.
💾
Your data
All data is securely stored in the cloud and private to your account. Sign in on any device to access it.
