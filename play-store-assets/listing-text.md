## Full description (max 4000 chars)
MyFinance is a straightforward personal finance tracker built for New Zealand households.

Import your bank transactions from a CSV file (Westpac, ANZ, BNZ, and ASB formats supported), automatically categorise them with your own rules, and see exactly where your money goes.

FEATURES
- Import transactions from your bank's CSV export, or add them manually
- Automatic categorisation with custom keyword rules
- Budgets vs actual spending, with fixed/variable breakdowns
- Interactive spending breakdown by category
- Profit & loss view by month, year, or year-to-date
- Track mortgages, debts, and payoff goals
- Track investments (KiwiSaver, shares, property) and net worth over time
- "Other Income" streams for a rental property, side business, or anything beyond your personal finances -- each with its own categories and budgets
- Share a household with a partner, with real-time sync across devices
- Light and dark theme
- Works offline, syncs when you're back online

Your data stays private to your household -- nothing is sold or shared with advertisers. See the full privacy policy at getmyfinance.app/privacy.html.

Some advanced features (unlimited history, cloud sync, household sharing) are part of an optional Pro plan, manageable from getmyfinance.app in your browser.
