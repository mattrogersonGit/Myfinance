MyFinance — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 20 July 2026

MyFinance is a personal finance tracking app operated by MJR Trades ("we", "us"). This policy explains what information MyFinance collects, how it's used, and who it's shared with. It applies to the web app and the Android app available on Google Play.

What we collect

MyFinance is built around the financial information you choose to enter. We collect:

  • Account information — your email address, used to sign in via Supabase Authentication.
  • Financial data you enter — bank transactions (imported by you from a CSV file or entered manually), account names and balances, budgets, categories, debts, mortgages, investments, and any household or "Other Income" streams you set up. None of this is imported automatically from your bank — you control what gets added.
  • Subscription status — if you upgrade to Pro, our payment processor (Stripe) shares your subscription status and a customer reference ID with us so we can unlock Pro features. We do not receive or store your card details — Stripe handles payment collection directly and is responsible for that data under its own privacy policy.

We don't run any advertising or third-party analytics/tracking scripts in the app.

How your data is stored

Your financial data is stored in a Supabase-hosted database (PostgreSQL) tied to your household account, and is also cached on your device (browser local storage) so the app keeps working offline. Access to your data in the database is restricted using row-level security so that only members of your own household can read or write it.

Household sharing

MyFinance lets you invite another person (e.g. a partner) to share your household's data. If you send or accept a household invite, both people can view and edit the same transactions, accounts, budgets, and other financial data in that household. Only invite people you're comfortable sharing this information with.

Who we share data with

We don't sell your data or share it with advertisers. Data is shared only with the service providers needed to run the app:

  • Supabase — database hosting and authentication.
  • Stripe — payment processing for Pro subscriptions.

We may also disclose information if required to by law.

Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account/household exists. You can permanently delete your own account and its data yourself at any time: sign in, go to Settings → Account, and choose Delete account. If you're the only member of your household, this deletes your login and all associated data (transactions, budgets, accounts, debts, investments, everything). If you share a household with someone else, it removes your access while their shared data stays intact. This action is immediate and cannot be undone.

If you'd rather we action the deletion for you, or you can no longer access the app, email us at the address below and we'll delete your account and data on request.

Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and database-level access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security.

Children's privacy

MyFinance is not directed at, or knowingly used to collect information from, children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after a change means you accept the updated policy.
